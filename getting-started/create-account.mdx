Ucode uses an authentication modal instead of separate signup pages. You can open it from **Get Started**, then switch to **Sign up** if the modal opens in login mode.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/ugen-sign-up.png" alt="Ucode create account modal" />
</Frame>

## Sign up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the authentication modal">
    Click **Get Started** in the top navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch to sign up">
    If the modal opens on the login screen, click **Sign up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter workspace details">
    Fill in **Company Name**, **User Login**, **Email**, and **Password**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the account">
    Click **Create Account** or choose **Sign up with Google**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Use your real company or team name. Ucode uses the company workspace to organize projects, billing, builders, and shared resources.
</Tip>

## Sign in

After the account is created, use **Sign in** with your login and password, or continue with Google if your account was created with Google.

## After signup

After signup, Ucode opens the workspace area. From there you can create a new chat, open Projects, review Chats, browse Templates, or switch workspaces. The first workspace is usually based on the company name entered during registration.

For team or client work, set the company name carefully because it becomes the workspace identity used for projects, plan limits, builders, and shared resources.

## Troubleshooting

- If the project builder opens the auth modal, sign in and submit the prompt again.
- If you cannot access a workspace, confirm that you are using the same login method you used during signup.
- If a teammate should access the same company workspace, invite them from workspace or user settings when available.
